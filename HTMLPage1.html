Formula 1 World Champions in the last 15 years

Getting to know about the sport


Formula 1 over the years has changed a lot ranging from number of races, teams and even the points system which is used to determine who would be champion.
It is a sport which has been around for about 75 years. This is the most watched automotive sport in Europe, Asia and it has been making its way into the USA.
Some of the countries which Formula 1 races at are:

  1. Austria
  2. Australia
  3. Germany
  4. Monaco

And some of the most famous tracks are:

  • Monaco
  • Abu Dhabi
  • Bahrain
  • Silverstone



For the last 15 years the championship has been won only by 5 drivers and these drivers are: Lewis Hamilton, Nico Rosberg, Sebastian Vettel , Jenson Button
and Max Verstappen. And only one of them has half of those 15 championships.

Lewis Hamilton has 7 World titles (joint-record and tied with Michael Schumacher) with 103 wins and pole positions and 192 podium finishes.
Nico Rosberg has 1 World title which he achieved in 2016 when he beat Lewis Hamilton with the same machinery.
Sebastian Vettel has 4 consecutive World Titles which he achieved in Red Bull Racing.
Jenson Button has 1 World title achieved with Brawn GP.
And the reigning Champion Max Verstappen has 2 World Titles which he achieved in 2021 and 2022.
