An honorable mention is the Championship of Braun GP's team which won both the Driver's and Constructor's Championship in 2009. The teams existed
only one year (2009) and in that year they won eight of the season's seventeen races, and by taking both titles in its only year of competition became the first
to achieve a 100% championship success rate.
Jenson Button won the Title with 95 points, 11 points ahead of Sebastian Vettel.
